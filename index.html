Tyler Menninga

Tyler Menninga is a driver for a monster truck called Grave Digger, which is owned by Monster Jam. There are many different events, although the main attractions are always the racing, two-wheel competitions, and freestyle competitions by monster trucks.

Small Town Beginnings

Tyler grew up in a small town in Iowa called Oskaloosa. Since he was young he has loved anything and everything to do with motocross sports. He started riding things like ATVs & four-wheelers at a very young age. Time spent not riding motorbikes or ATVs was spent playing video games or watching his favorite athletes drive monster trucks. His father knew people in the industry on a personal level, which turned out to be the catalyst to getting his foot in the door into the Monster Jam world when he graduated high school. He showed a lot of enthusiasm when talking in length to recruiters for Monster Jam and was asked to try out.

The Journey Begins

After trying out, the recruiters for Monster Jam were impressed and Tyler accepted a job offer. He went to Monster Jam University in Wisconsin, which is a large area for new Monster Jam drivers to go to upon signing on to receive training. This training includes how to operate the truck and various equipment, how to speak to press and do interviews, and manage a social media profile.

Tyler started out driving for Blue Thunder. He performed in various events and tournaments, and quickly garnered a dedicated fanbase. After a couple years of driving Blue Thunder and making a name for himself, he switched teams to drive for Grave Digger, marking a high point in his early career. He continued to gain fans and win competitions, he is most notably known for being a repeated winner of the Triple Threat Championship Series of events which include racing and freestyle. Things were put on hold for a while due to the Coronavirus Pandemic, though Monster Jam drivers still do interviews and connect with their fans online.
